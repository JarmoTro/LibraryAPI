Viewing, creating, editing, and deleting reviews

Here you can find out how to view, add, edit, and delete reviews
NB: You can only edit and delete your own reviews. Admins can delete all reviews
NB: You have to be logged in. Find out how to login here
  • Viewing reviews
  • Creating reviews
  • Editing reviews
  • Deleting reviews

Viewing reviews

Click on a book you want to view the reviews of

Near the bottom of the page you can see the books reviews

Creating reviews

Click on a book you want to create the review for

Click on the "Add review" button

Fill out the form and press the "Add review" button

Editing reviews

Click on a book you want to edit the review for (or find the review in your user page)

Choose a review you want to edit and click the edit icon

Fill out the form and click "Edit review"

Deleting reviews

Click on a book you want to edit the review for (or find the review in your user page)

Choose a review you want to delete and click the delete icon

Click on "Delete review"
