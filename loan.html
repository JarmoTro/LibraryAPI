Viewing, creating, editing, and deleting loans

Here you can find out how to view, add, edit, and delete loans
NB: You have to be logged in and you must have admin privileges. Find out how to login here and how to grant privileges here

Click on the admin dropdown button and select admin panel

Here you can see 8 upcoming loans

You can search for a specific users loans by filling out the form and clicking "Find loans"

Click on a book you want to loan out

Click the "Create a loan" button

Fill out the form and click "Create". After creating the loan, the books stock will be reduced by 1

Click on the admin dropdown button and select admin panel

Choose a loan you want to edit and click the edit icon

Fill out the form and click "Update"

Click on the admin dropdown button and select admin panel

Choose a loan you want to delete and click the delete icon

To delete the loan click the "Delete" button. After deleting the loan, the books stock will be increase by 1
